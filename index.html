Concentrico Architecture Competition

2024 | Spain

Oasis del Aire - The Proposal project consists of tree-shaped modules to create urban oases in areas impacted by global warming. With a lightweight metal structure and a tensioned canvas roof, the modules filter sunlight and reduce heat through moss and programmed water vaporization. Adjustable to the height of local buildings, these modules form a harmonious mesh, integrating with the landscaping and balancing air humidity while storing rainwater.
